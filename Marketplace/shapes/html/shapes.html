SHAPES

Create and edit domain content in VR.

Hands

  • Move an entity: Grab it with a hand or laser.
  • Stretch an entity: Grab it with both hands or lasers.
  • Delete an entity: Grab it and squeeze the grip.

Tools Menu

  • Activate a tool: Click its button. Its icon is attached to your hand.
  • Deactivate a tool: Hover and click the title bar or squeeze the grip.

Create Palette

Click an icon and the entity is created at your hand. It is grabbed if you continue to squeeze the trigger.
